Return Policy

Customer Satisfaction is our top most priority thus We are happy to offer a 7 day, return policy (with full refund or credit) for unused and unworn items that still have the tag attached. The only exception is for SALE items.Sale items are not returnable.

In case of returns, customers are responsible to ship the product back to us in an undamaged condition with all tags intact.Return shipping charges are to be borne by customer.
